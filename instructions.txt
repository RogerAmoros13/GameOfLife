----------------------------- CONWAY'S GAME OF LIFE -------------------------------

RULES
- If a cell has 2 or 3 neighbour cells, it survives.
- If a dead cell has 3 neighbour cells, it revives.

INSTRUCTIONS
- Left click creates an alive cell.
- Right click kills an alive cell.
- When the distribution of alive cells is done, press SPACE for runnin Game Of Life.
- Press r for kill every cell.
